I rebuilt this site. The old version was a Jekyll academic-pages template from
grad school — publications, talks, the whole CV-as-a-website format. It served
its purpose, but it wasn't a great fit for what I actually want to use this
space for: writing, and showing off things I build.

So this is a fresh start — [Astro](https://astro.build), Markdown for posts,
Tailwind for styling. Nothing fancy, just a landing page, a blog, and a place
to put projects.

More posts to come.
